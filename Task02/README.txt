This application solves the following tasks:
1)Check if 3 numbers are equal or not.
2)Converts weight in kilograms to milligrams, grams and tons.
3)Find the area of the ring whose outer radius is R1, and the inner radius is R2.
4)Check that numbers of the four-digit number N form an increasing (decreasing) sequence.
5)Finds average arithmetic and geometric of digits of the six-digit number.
6)Makes the reverse of a seven-digit number.
7)Swap two values without using other variables.
