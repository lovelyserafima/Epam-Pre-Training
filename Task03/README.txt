This application solves the following tasks:
1)Check if 3 points (x,y) of Cartesian plane can make an triangle. If it is true, check if this triangle is rectangular.
2)At a young age, a dragon grows three heads each year, but after he will be 200 years old - only two, and after 300 years - only one. 
It is assumed that the dragon appears in the light with three heads. Calculate the number of heads and eyes of N-year dragon.
3)Determine whether the letter (symbol) is a vowel letter. Solve it at least by 4 methods.
4)Generate random number which corresponds to any smile.
5)Returns the next day of entered date.
